Who are acid reign?

We are a British thrash metal band formed in 1985. The group was active until 1991, when they disbanded. Returned in 2015 with a rebooted line up.


Howard "H" Smith – vocals
Cooky – guitar
Paul Chanter – guitar
Pete Dee – bass
Marc Jackson – drums
